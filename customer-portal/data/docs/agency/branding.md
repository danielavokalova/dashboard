# Branding & Logo

<!-- tags: branding, logo, colour, design, white-label, appearance -->

Customise the visual identity of your booking engine so customers see your brand.

## Logo

1. Go to **Settings → Agency → Branding**.
2. Click **Upload logo**.
3. Supported formats: PNG, SVG. Recommended size: 300 × 80 px.
4. Click **Save**.

## Colours

Set your primary and secondary brand colours (hex code).  
These apply to buttons, headers and highlights in the customer-facing booking flow.

## Preview

Use the **Preview** button to see how your branding looks before publishing.
