# Agency Profile

<!-- tags: agency, profile, name, address, contact, IATA, TIDS -->

Your agency profile contains the basic identification data displayed in bookings, invoices and emails.

## What you can edit

- **Agency name** – official trading name
- **Address** – street, city, country, ZIP
- **Contact email & phone** – used in customer-facing communications
- **IATA / TIDS number** – required for GDS ticketing
- **VAT number** – printed on invoices

## How to update

1. Go to **Settings → Agency → Profile**.
2. Edit the required fields.
3. Click **Save changes**.

> ⚠️ Changes to IATA/TIDS number require verification by GOL support before they take effect.
